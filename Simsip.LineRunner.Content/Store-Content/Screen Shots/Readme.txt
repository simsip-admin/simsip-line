Screen shots
Google play requires a minimum of two and a maximum of eight screen shots for an application. 
They will be displayed on an application’s details page in Google Play.

The specs for screen shots are:
24 bit PNG or JPG with no alpha channel
320w x 480h 
OR 
480w x 800h 
OR 
480w x 854h. 

Landscaped images will be cropped.
